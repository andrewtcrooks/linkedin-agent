# ICP Scoring (Data Nova Consulting)

Use a 0–10 score.

## Firmographic fit (0–4)

- 0: Wrong segment (enterprise-only, solo creator, unrelated org type)
- 2: Partial fit
- 4: Strong fit (small business, typically 5–50 employees)

## Pain signal fit (0–3)

Look for signs of manual ops friction:

- Repetitive admin work
- Scheduling/dispatch chaos
- Lead follow-up gaps
- Multi-tool copy/paste workflows
- Team bottlenecks from owner dependency

## Buyer-fit (0–2)

- 0: Individual contributor with no buying influence
- 1: Possible influencer
- 2: Owner/operator/decision-maker

## Timing/trigger (0–1)

- 0: No clear urgency
- 1: Recent trigger (hiring growth, expansion, new process pain mention, active ops discussions)

## Qualification threshold

- 7–10: qualified
- 5–6: nurture / second pass
- <5: discard
