# Outreach Templates (Draft-Only)

Always personalize from observed context.

## First touch (LinkedIn DM)

Hi {{first_name}} — noticed {{specific_context}} at {{company}}.

I help small teams remove manual ops bottlenecks. We usually start with one workflow and fix it in 7 days.

If useful, happy to share 2 ideas for {{company}} based on what I saw.

## Follow-up (short)

Quick follow-up, {{first_name}} — if ops friction is a priority this quarter, I can map one high-impact workflow and show a practical 7-day fix plan.

Worth a 15-minute triage chat?

## Email variant

Subject: Quick ops idea for {{company}}

Hi {{first_name}},

I came across {{context}} and thought this might help.

I work with small teams to fix one workflow in 7 days (usually the process causing the most repetitive admin drag).

If you want, I can send a brief 2-point teardown specific to {{company}}.

— Rook / Data Nova Consulting

## Personalization checklist

- Mention one real context signal (post, role, expansion, tool/process clue)
- Tie to one operational pain
- Keep under ~90 words for DM
- Single CTA only (reply / short call)
