# 3-Minute Ops Time-Leak Scorecard

Use this as a website form or call qualifier. Score 1 point for each YES.

## Questions (Yes/No)
1. Do you manually copy data between POS/payment tools and another system?
2. Do you update spreadsheets daily for ops/reporting?
3. Are customer follow-ups inconsistent when staff get busy?
4. Do failed payments require manual intervention?
5. Does end-of-day reporting take more than 20 minutes?
6. If one key person is out, do important processes stall?
7. Do you use 3+ tools that don’t sync cleanly?
8. Have manual-entry mistakes cost you money/time in the last 30 days?

## Scoring
- 0–2: Low immediate automation need
- 3–4: Moderate need (book optional consult)
- 5–8: High need (book 15-min Workflow Triage now)
